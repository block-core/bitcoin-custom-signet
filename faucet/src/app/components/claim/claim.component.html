About Bitcoin Testnet

The Bitcoin Testnet is a parallel blockchain used for testing purposes. It functions just like the main Bitcoin network but with test Bitcoins, which hold no real-world value. Developers and enthusiasts use the Testnet to test applications, experiment with Bitcoin transactions, or learn without risking real Bitcoins.

Important Note: Testnet Bitcoins are purely for testing and development. They cannot be traded, sold, or used for real payments.

Use this faucet responsibly to claim small amounts of test Bitcoins for your experiments. Abuse of the faucet may result in restrictions.
